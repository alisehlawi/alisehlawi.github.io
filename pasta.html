Oven-baked pasta     Shish tawook     Garlic bread

Ingredients

    • Two chicken breasts, sliced into cubes
      • Seasoning:
      • 1 large spoonful of vinegar
      • Ground red pepper
      • Ground paprika
      • Garlic powder
    • Fusilli pasta
    • Two cloves of garlic (ground)
    • One large onion (ground)
    • Ground green chilli
    • Fresh mushroom (sliced)
    • 2 large spoonfuls of tomato paste
    • 3 large tomatoes, ground in a blender
    • One cup of cooking cream
    • Grated mozarella cheese
    • Spices to taste (salt, black pepper, cumin powder, etc.)

    Preparation

    1. Place pasta in pot of boiling water, making sure the pasta is engulfed. Add a teaspoon of salt, stir, and leave it to simmer on a medium flame.
    2. In another large pot, on a medium flame, add some cooking oil, the ground onion, garlic, and chilli, then add the tomato paste and mix well.
    3. Season the chicken.
    4. Add the sliced chicken to the mixture, and keep mixing until the chicken turns white.
    5. Add the sliced mushrooms and the rest of the prepared spices to mixture. Continue mixing for a few minutes.
    6. Add 1/2 a cup of the water used to cook the pasta to the mixture, and stir for a few minutes.
    7. Add the blended tomato mixture, and continue stirring.
    8. Add the cup of cooking cream, stir, and then let the mixture simmer on a low flame for 10 minutes. In the meantime, preheat the oven , setting it to a high temperature.
    9. The pasta should be ready by now, so extinguish the flame and drain the water from the pasta using a sieve.
    10. Extinguish the flame, and add the pasta to the mixture. Mix well.
    11. Add the mixture to a baking dish, and cover it with a layer of grated mozarella cheese until the mixture can no longer be seen.
    12. Place the dish into the oven for approximately 15 minutes. When the cheese layer is thouroughly cooked (with some brown spots forming), remove from oven - the dish is ready to be served!
