Oven-baked pasta     Shish tawook     Garlic bread

Ingredients

  • One baguette
  • Salted butter (room temperature)
  • Three cloves of garlic
  • Parsley (ground)
  • Grated mozarella cheese
  • Grated cheddar cheese

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius.
  2. Evenly slice the baguette, slicing diagonally.
  3. Mix 3 tablespoons of salted butter with the ground parsley.
  4. Slather one side of the sliced baguette pieces with the butter mixture.
  5. Add an even amount of mozarella and cheddar to the buttered side of the bread.
  6. Place the prepared garlic bread on a baking tray, and place into oven.
  7. When the cheese melts and begins to turn a golden colour, remove the tray from the oven.
  8. Your garlic bread is ready to serve!
