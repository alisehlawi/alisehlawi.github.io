3 Easy but DELICIOUS Recipes!

Looking to delight yourself and your loved ones with some amazing cooking, but never actually been any good at it? Find yourself looking for recipes online, only to be met with ingredients you've never heard of before, and instructions you struggle to understand? Well, look no further! These 3 recipes are simple to follow, look great once finished, and taste even better!

Oven-baked pasta
A delicious pasta with chicken and mushroom in a spicy, cheesy, sauce, baked under a layer of mozarella cheese. Perfect to serve for lunch!
Shish tawook
A traditional Arabic recipe and time-proven classic, this unique way to prepare and season your chicken shish is bound to delight friends and family at any gathering!
Garlic bread
If you're looking for a side dish rather than something to serve for the main course, look no further. Its name is self-explanatory, and its taste is unrivaled. Learn how to prepare this delicious starter yourself rather than relying on fast food joints!
